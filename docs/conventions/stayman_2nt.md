## 2n-(p)-3c = Stayman

### Opener's Responses

- **3d** = No 4-card major
  - **3h** = Smolen, 4 hearts + 5 spades, game forcing
    - **3s** = 3 spades, sets trump
    - **3n** = No 3 spades, to play
    - **4s** = 3 spades, to play
  - **3s** = Smolen, 4 spades + 5 hearts, game forcing
    - **3n** = No 3 hearts, to play
    - **4h** = 3 hearts, to play
  - **3n** = Signoff
  - **4c** = 5+ clubs, slam interest
  - **4d** = 5+ diamonds, slam interest
  - **4h** = 6 hearts + 4 spades, no slam interest
  - **4s** = 6 spades + 4 hearts, no slam interest
  - **4n** = Invitational to 6NT

- **3h** = 4+ hearts, may have 4 spades
  - **3s** = Slam try, 4+ hearts
  - **3n** = To play, 4 spades (no heart fit)
  - **4c** = 5+ clubs, slam interest
  - **4d** = 5+ diamonds, slam interest
  - **4h** = To play
  - **4n** = Invitational to 6NT

- **3s** = 4+ spades, denies 4 hearts
  - **3n** = To play, 4 hearts (no spade fit)
  - **4c** = 5+ clubs, slam interest
  - **4d** = 5+ diamonds, slam interest
  - **4h** = Slam try, 4+ spades
  - **4s** = To play
  - **4n** = Invitational to 6NT
