# XYZ

Checkback convention after any 1x - 1y - 1z auction (auction still at 1-level after opener's rebid).

---

## Applies After

- 1c-(p)-1d-(p)-1h
- 1c-(p)-1d-(p)-1s
- 1c-(p)-1d-(p)-1n
- 1c-(p)-1h-(p)-1s
- 1c-(p)-1h-(p)-1n
- 1c-(p)-1s-(p)-1n
- 1d-(p)-1h-(p)-1s
- 1d-(p)-1h-(p)-1n
- 1d-(p)-1s-(p)-1n

---

  - All responses natural
  - **2M** = 3-card support if responder bid that major
