## 1n-(p)-2c = Stayman

### Opener's Responses

- **2d** = No 4-card major
  - **p** = To play
  - **2h** = Pass or correct, weak with both majors
    - **p** = 3 hearts, to play
    - **2s** = 2 hearts, 3 spades, to play
  - **2n** = Invitational, no 4-card major
  - **3c** = 5+ clubs, game forcing
  - **3d** = 5+ diamonds, game forcing
  - **3h** = Smolen, 4 hearts + 5 spades, game forcing
    - **3s** = 3 spades, sets trump
    - **3n** = No 3 spades, to play
    - **4s** = 3 spades, minimum
  - **3s** = Smolen, 4 spades + 5 hearts, game forcing
    - **3n** = No 3 hearts, to play
    - **4h** = 3 hearts, minimum
  - **3n** = Signoff, no major fit
  - **4n** = Invitational to 6NT

- **2h** = 4+ hearts, may have 4 spades
  - **p** = To play, weak with hearts
  - **2s** = Invitational, 4 spades
    - **2n** = Minimum, no 4 spades
    - **3h** = Minimum, 4 hearts only
    - **3s** = 4-4 majors, minimum
    - **3n** = Maximum, no 4 spades
    - **4h** = Maximum, 4 hearts only
    - **4s** = 4-4 majors, maximum
  - **2n** = Invitational, denies 4 spades
  - **3c** = 5+ clubs, game forcing
  - **3d** = 5+ diamonds, game forcing
  - **3h** = Invitational, 4 hearts
    - **p** = Minimum
    - **4h** = Maximum
  - **3s** = Slam try, 4+ hearts
  - **3n** = To play, 4 spades (no heart fit)
  - **4h** = To play, 4+ hearts
  - **4n** = Invitational to 6NT

- **2s** = 4+ spades, denies 4 hearts
  - **p** = To play, weak with spades
  - **2n** = Invitational
  - **3c** = 5+ clubs, game forcing
  - **3d** = 5+ diamonds, game forcing
  - **3h** = Slam try, 4+ spades
  - **3s** = Invitational, 4 spades
    - **p** = Minimum
    - **4s** = Maximum
  - **3n** = To play, 4 hearts (no spade fit)
  - **4s** = To play, 4+ spades
  - **4n** = Invitational to 6NT
